  • Training and resource development
Ongoing management starts at $1,500/month.

How long does it take to launch?

Most programs launch in 4-6 weeks:

  • Weeks 1-2: Strategy & setup
  • Weeks 3-4: Technology implementation
  • Weeks 5-6: Initial partner onboarding
First results typically appear within 60-90 days.

What commission structure works best?

We recommend:

  • Recurring commissions for SaaS (20-30% of first payment, 10-20% recurring)
  • Tiered commissions based on performance volume
  • Hybrid models combining flat fees with percentage commissions
We'll help you design the optimal model for your business.

How do you find quality partners?

We use multiple acquisition channels:

  • Existing customer database mining
  • Targeted outreach to industry influencers
  • Partnerships with complementary service providers
  • Affiliate directories and marketplaces
  • Referral programs for current partners
