FAQ

How do you select the right creators?

We use a proprietary scoring system that evaluates:

  • Audience demographics match
  • Engagement quality (not just quantity)
  • Content relevance to your industry
  • Historical campaign performance
  • Audience authenticity metrics

What's the typical campaign duration?

Most effective campaigns run 60-90 days to allow for content creation, distribution, and performance analysis. We recommend starting with a 3-month pilot program.

How do you measure ROI?

We track:

  • Attributed revenue using custom tracking links
  • Engagement rates and audience sentiment
  • Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
  • Brand lift through pre/post-campaign surveys
  • Long-term customer value from creator-referred users

Do you offer performance-based pricing?

Yes, we have hybrid models that combine management fees with performance bonuses based on achieved results.
