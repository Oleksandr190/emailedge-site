Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How quickly can we expect to see results?

Most clients see initial traction within 30-45 days, with significant growth by month 3. We implement rapid testing cycles to identify winning strategies quickly. Our phased approach ensures we build momentum while laying the foundation for sustainable growth. Typically, we see:

  • First 30 days: Strategy development and initial testing
  • 30-60 days: Optimization and scaling of winning channels
  • 60-90 days: Significant ROI and growth acceleration
What's your minimum engagement period?

We require a minimum 3-month engagement to properly research, implement, and optimize growth strategies. Most clients see the best ROI after 6+ months and choose to extend our partnership. Our typical engagement timeline:

  • 3 months: Initial growth acceleration
  • 6 months: Sustainable scaling
  • 12 months: Market leadership positioning
Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes, we specialize in scaling SaaS companies from $10K to $1M+ ARR. We have specific packages designed for startups with limited budgets. Our startup program includes:

  • Product-market fit validation
  • Early adopter acquisition strategies
  • Growth roadmap development
  • Founder training on growth fundamentals
  • Performance-based pricing options
How do you measure success?

We define KPIs during onboarding and track metrics like CAC, LTV, MRR growth, and conversion rates. You'll receive detailed performance reports monthly. Our success metrics include:

  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) reduction
  • Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) growth
  • Lead-to-customer conversion rate improvement
  • Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) increase
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
